# ContentCued Brand Guidelines

## Voice & Tone

- **Friendly & Approachable:** We speak like a helpful tech-savvy friend
- **Trustworthy:** We're reliable, transparent, and honest
- **Expert yet Accessible:** We know AI but explain it simply
- **Enthusiastic:** We're excited about AI and share that excitement

#### Instagram

- **Tone:** Visual, trendy, aesthetic
- **Style:** More polished, emoji use moderate
- **CTA:** WhatsApp link in bio/below post
- **Character Limit:** <2200 characters
- **Hashtags:** 10-15 hashtags (mix of broad and niche)

**Example:**

- **Warning Orange:** #F59E0B
- **Error Red:** #EF4444

### Background Colors
- **Dark Mode BG:** #0F172A (Slate 900)
- **Light Mode BG:** #FFFFFF

### Usage Guidelines
- Primary blue should be dominant in all visuals
- Use gradient accents for CTAs and highlights
- Dark backgrounds preferred for tech/modern aesthetic
- Maintain good contrast for accessibility (WCAG AA minimum)

## Typography

### Headlines
- **Font:** Inter, Poppins, or Noto Sans Bengali
- **Weight:** 600-700 (Semi-Bold to Bold)
- **Case:** Title Case for English

### Body Copy
- **Font:** Inter or Noto Sans Bengali
- **Weight:** 400-500 (Regular to Medium)
- **Size:** 14-16px for digital, 12-14px for mobile

### Bangla Text
- **Font:** Noto Sans Bengali (must support Bangla script)
- **Rendering:** Ensure proper ligature display
- **Mixed Script:** When mixing Bangla+English, use Noto Sans Bengali

## Brand Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ DO

- **Use BDT pricing prominently** in all content
- **Include WhatsApp CTA** in Facebook/Instagram posts
- **Mix Bangla and English** naturally per platform guidelines
- **Show the price clearly** (e.g., "৳2,500/month")
- **Mention instant activation** as a key benefit
- **Use the brand blue** (#1A365D, #3B82F6) in visuals
- **Include emojis** for Facebook/Instagram (🔥⚡💰🇧🇩✨🚀)
- **Reference local payment** (bKash, Nagad)
- **Be enthusiastic** and positive
- **Highlight use cases** for each product

### ❌ DON'T

- **NEVER mention G2A, reselling, keys, or cracks** — Critical brand safety rule
- **Don't use excessive English** on Facebook/WhatsApp (keep it Bangla-heavy)
- **Don't skip the CTA** — every post must have a clear next step
- **Don't use generic stock photos** — use modern tech visuals
- **Don't make false claims** about features or pricing
- **Don't ignore Bangla typography** — ensure proper rendering
- **Don't use more than 15 hashtags** on Instagram
- **Don't post without WhatsApp link** on Facebook/Instagram
- **Don't use only English on LinkedIn** — 10-20% Bangla shows local presence
- **Don't forget the "instant activation" benefit** — key differentiator

## Bangla/English Mixing Rules

### When to Use Bangla
- Pricing and payment terms (৳2,500, বিকাশ, নগদ)
- Emotional appeals (ধন্যবাদ, অসাধারণ, সীমিত সময়)
- Local cultural references (ঈদ, পহেলা বৈশাখ)
- Conversational phrases (কেমন আছেন, কি খবর)

### When to Use English
- Product names (ChatGPT Plus, Midjourney)
- Technical terms (API, AI, Subscription)
- Feature names (GPT-4, Image Generation)
- Professional contexts (LinkedIn, business messaging)

### Mixing Patterns
- **Facebook:** "অর্ডার করুন" instead of "Order Now"
- **Instagram:** "Unlock AI Superpowers" with Bangla explanation
- **LinkedIn:** English with 1-2 Bangla words for local relevance
- **WhatsApp:** Bangla with English product names and technical terms

## Visual Style Guidelines

### Design Principles
- **Modern & Clean:** Minimal clutter, focus on key message
- **Tech-Forward:** Gradients, glowing effects, dark backgrounds
- **Bangladesh Identity:** Use flag colors (red/green) subtly
- **Price-First:** Make BDT price the most prominent element

### Image Composition
- **Center Focus:** Product name or price in center
- **Background:** Dark gradient (#1A365D → #0F172A)
- **Accent:** Blue to purple gradient overlay
- **Text Overlay:** White text with subtle shadow
- **Brand Element:** ContentCued logo or name in corner

### Graphic Templates
1. **Product Showcase:** Product name + price + key benefit
2. **Comparison:** Before/After or With/Without pricing
3. **Testimonial:** Customer quote + profile picture
4. **Feature Highlight:** Single feature with icon
5. **Urgency:** Countdown timer or "Limited Time" badge
